Life Operating System

Operating System for Conscious Awareness and Response

Olos AI (and Oscar, the Paradigm Dragon) is a long-term project to build a personal AI system that actually lives with you – across your health, work, home, and future plans – instead of locking your data away in someone else's cloud.

What you’re seeing now is just the first public node. Behind this page, the foundations are being laid: local AI, privacy-first memory, and tools designed for real humans with real lives – not enterprise dashboards.

Ethos
  • Privacy first. Your data should serve you, not be harvested from you.
  • Local when possible. Cloud is optional, not mandatory.
  • Human-scale design. Built for actual daily life, not for hype.
  • Built to grow. From “Baby Oscar” to full Life Operating System, one step at a time.
